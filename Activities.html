Our Club Activities

From debate stages to storytelling nights, our activities are designed to challenge and inspire.

Home Activities
01. Competitive contest

Debate Competition

Challenge your thinkings, your thoughts and opinions. Argue your points and opinions to the oposing size.Clearly state your stand on the real-world topics.The test of logic, speed, and persuasion.

Core Skills You'll Master:

  • Critical Thinking: solve complex arguments instantly.
  • Refutation Mechanics: Learn to spot logic gaps under pressure.
  • Teamwork: Collaborate with teammates to debate the topic.
Every Tuesday & Thursday 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM Register for Session
02. Speaking development

Public Speaking

Stand in front of many audiences, deliver your speech smoothly and confidently. Develop confident speaking skills which are important for personal development. Every great leader started exactly where you are.

Core Skills You'll Master:

  • Speech Delivery & Hooks: Hook your audience inside the first 10 seconds.
  • Body Language: Master staging spaces, gestures, and clear vocal presence.
  • Confidence skill: Overcome stage fright by using the tactics learned.
Every Thursday Session 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM Register for Session
03. Creative Expression

Storytelling Contest

Let your imagination run wild. Talk and perform the stories that captivate, inspire, and leave your audience in awe. Because the best stories are not just told, they are felt, remembered, and lived.

Core Skills You'll Master:

  • Creativity & Plot Hooks: Leave your audience in awe mesmerizing.
  • Pronunciation & Voice Acting: Adjust vocal tune and accent to attract audience
  • Expression Control: Make the expression which attractive like the music to the ear.
Monthly Showcase 3:30 PM - 6:00 PM Register for Session
