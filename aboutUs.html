Our Story

A Club Born From a Passion for Language

The English Horizon was founded by a small group of students who believed that language is the most powerful tool for connection, growth, and opportunity.
Over the years we have hosted many events, taught hundreds of students, and watched shy first-timers change into confident public speakers. Our journey is to make sure
people have confident and connection with other, and our club is still just beginning.

Today the club stands as a motivation and inspiring place where every student, regardless of their current English level, is welcomed, challenged, and celebrated.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision &Values

Our Mission

To provide students with a dynamic, good environment where they can practise English, build communication skills, and grow as confident individuals ready to face the world.

Our Vision

A school community where every student can express themselves clearly and boldly in English — unlocking doors to higher education, careers, and global friendships.

Our values

We believe in respect, and continuous growth. Every member is encouraged to speak up, support one another, and embrace the courage it takes to learn something new.
